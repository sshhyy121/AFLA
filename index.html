COMPANY INTRODUCTION

Qingdao Asiafrila Steel Structure CO., Ltd is a professional steel structure company with special qualification of steel structure. Located in Konggang industrial park and established in 2019, we mainly specialized in light and heavy steel structure designing, manufacturing and installing.

With 3 factories and a floor area 26,000 m², workshop area 22,000 m², we own the light H steel production line, C/Z purlin production line and various types of profile sheet machines.
